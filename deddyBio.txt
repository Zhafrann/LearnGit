Deodatus Andreas Deddy Cahyadi Sunjoyo, 
commonly known as Deddy Corbuzier, is an Indonesian mentalist, professional illusionist, and television presenter. 
He is a recipient of the Merlin Award. 
He is actively creating video on his Youtube channel mainly podcast known as “Close the Door”.

He made his debut on television in 1998 with Impresario RCTI. 
He did many publicity stunts with many famous magicians.
He is also known as a YouTuber. 
As of September 2020 his YouTube channel has over 10.8 million subscribers and his videos have over 1.7 billion views.
